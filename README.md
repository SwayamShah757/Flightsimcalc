# Flightsimcalc
A Quick Time calculator to your destination from whatever point in your flightpath, for when you are playing Flight Simulators.
Step 1: Input the Distance in NM to your Destination
Step 2: Put your Ground Speed or True Airspeed into the field for speed
Step 3: Click the Calculate button and get a rough estimate of how much time it will take for you to arrive at your destination
Super helpful if you have a tight schedule but still want to have time for flight simming
